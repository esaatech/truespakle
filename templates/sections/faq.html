Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive cleaning services including home cleaning, office cleaning, deep cleaning, and move in/out cleaning services.

How do you price your services?

Our pricing is based on the size of the space, type of service required, and frequency of cleaning. Contact us for a free customized quote.

Are your cleaning products eco-friendly?

Yes, we use environmentally friendly cleaning products that are safe for your family, pets, and the environment while maintaining excellent cleaning results.

How often should I schedule cleaning services?

This depends on your needs. We offer weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, and one-time cleaning services to accommodate different schedules and requirements.

Are your cleaners insured and bonded?

Yes, all our cleaning professionals are fully insured and bonded. We also conduct thorough background checks for your peace of mind.

What's included in a deep cleaning service?

Deep cleaning includes detailed attention to all areas, including baseboards, window sills, behind appliances, inside cabinets, and thorough sanitization of bathrooms and kitchens.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

No, you don't need to be home. Many clients provide a key or access code. We ensure secure handling of all access methods.

We request 24-hour notice for cancellations. Late cancellations may incur a fee. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to reschedule.

Do you bring your own cleaning supplies?

Yes, we bring all necessary cleaning supplies and equipment. If you prefer specific products, we're happy to use them upon request.

What's your satisfaction guarantee?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied, we'll return to address any areas of concern at no additional cost.
